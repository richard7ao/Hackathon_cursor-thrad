Buy-Side Agents

The human-in-the-loop question: when can the agent commit money alone, what counts as a brand-safety violation, and who signs off on creative before it serves?

Auto-bids on conversational intent, escalates spend spikes

Sell-Side & Measurement
Agents that help AI publishers monetize and prove ROI — picking placements, scoring prompt-level intent, attributing conversions back to chat sessions. The risk is indirect: a bad signal becomes a bad billing event. The human-in-the-loop question: when is intent strong enough to monetize, and which conversions need a human-confirmed audit?

Scores prompt intent in real time, gates ad eligibility
Stitches attribution from chat → click → conversion
Flags suspicious traffic, asks before refunding spend
Build for the publisher / SSP / measurement side: AI apps, chatbots, attribution platforms.
